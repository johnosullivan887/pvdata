Welcome to PVData

PVData is an open, interactive database of perovskite–silicon tandem solar cells. Explore device efficiencies, materials, layer architectures, and indium use from the literature. An interactive companion to our review paper, showcasing its figures and underlying databases: Transparent Conducting Electrodes for Perovskite--Silicon Tandem Solar Cells, Nature Reviews Physics 8, 344–364 (2026) .
